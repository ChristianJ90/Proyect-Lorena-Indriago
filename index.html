Services

Microblading service

Microblading service

Microblading is a semi-permanent makeup technique, whose objective is to completely correct or reconstruct an eyebrow lacking or absent hair, making hair in an artistic way, creating a hyper-realistic and natural effect.

Acrylic Nail Service

Acrylic Nail Service

She wears beautiful acrylic nail extensions, whose objective is give our nail a better appearance and protect it from the damaging effects of enamels and other agents.

Hair removal service with thread

Hair removal service with thread

The threading technique is a totally natural procedure, in which the beautician uses a loop of thread that can be either 100% cotton or silk.

Eyelash extensions

Eyelash extensions

It is a placement of false eyelashes that are made on each hair of your eye. In this way, you will be able to lengthen your eyelashes in a very natural way and with a duration that is semi-permanent. (Different types of fans are handled).

Facial Cleansing

Facial Cleansing

It consists of thoroughly cleaning the skin, moisturizing it and protecting it. Helps remove traces of dirt, makeup, sebum, pollution and dead skin cells.

lip micropigmentation

lip micropigmentation

Lip micropigmentation is a treatment that consists of micro incisions in which pigments are implanted at the epidermal level and is performed with small needles. It is a process very similar to eyebrow microblading, although it requires greater delicacy due to the sensitivity of the area.
